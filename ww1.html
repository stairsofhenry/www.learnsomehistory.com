WW1

Countries that were involved

WW1 was a conflict that lasted from 1914 to 1918. It was between the allies and the Central Powers. The Allies included Britain, France, Russia, Serbia, Japan, Portugal, Italy, and much later [in 1917], the United States. The Central Powers included Germany, the Austro-Hungarian Empire AKA Austria-Hungary, Bulgaria, and the Ottoman Empire.
